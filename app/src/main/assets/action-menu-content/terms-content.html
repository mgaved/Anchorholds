Disclaimer

Please note that the Anchorholds app is a research prototype and not a fully advanced product. Thus, we cannot guarantee accurate functionality the whole time. You use the app at your own risk.

Use of data

We will not share data generated by using Anchorholds with anyone for any reason apart from adherence to national laws. Your data will be stored anonymously and only used within the MAZI project.

The Bluetooth LE beacons placed in the environment that your phone interacts with gather no information and have no memory of which users' devices have been within range of them.
