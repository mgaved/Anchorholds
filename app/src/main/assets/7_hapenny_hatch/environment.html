Environment

This is one of the best locations to view the creek and gain an impression of all the activities going on around at once. Ahead the DLR glides across the rising apartment towers on the old Faircharm site. Below, the creek at lowtide wanders out to the Thames through muddy banks and the gravel and rubble of the river bottom.
