Environment

The OneSE8 estate on the other side of the rails here, was the first of so many new apartment developments in the area. It's builders underestimated the effect of weathering on the exterior finishes and roofing construction leaving tenants to fight for repairs and refurbishment yet it's less than 15 years old!.
