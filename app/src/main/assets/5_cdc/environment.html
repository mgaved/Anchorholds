Environment

A voice for nature conservation and biodiversity in the area. Creekside Education Trust (CET) is committed to the long-term management of Deptford Creek for people and wildlife. CET offers ecological planning advice for Creek wall improvements and local habitats to developers and partners. If you are interested in finding out more about this service, please contact the Creekside Discovery Centre.
