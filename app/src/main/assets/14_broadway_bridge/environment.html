Environment

This area is part of the Waterlink Way linking up Deptford to Lewisham, Catford and Sydenham. The trail follows the creek and where it merges with the River Ravensbourne.

As the creek goes underneath the Deptford DLR station on the Broadway it meets the River Ravensbourne. Read council plans from 2010 for the river.
