This newly built swing bridge crosses the creek at Millennium Quay as Deptford Creek tips into the Thames. It links the river pathways from Deptford and Greenwich and makes way for the Aggregate ship of JJ Prior each day at high tide.

Check the PLA tide timetable for high tide times to catch bridge swing open to allow river traffic to pass.
