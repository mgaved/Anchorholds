Activities

Activity 1

Choose some sentences from a book or a newspaper. Notice the position of the adverbs or adverbial phrases (e.g. time expressions, like ‘last week’). Rewrite the sentences with the adverbs in a different place. Check with a friend to see if your sentences are correct.

Activity 2 - internet only!

Read this description of the Network Railcard.

  • How long does the railcard last?
  • What is the word/phrase that means ‘pay for’ the £30 you spend on the railcard?

  • Do you always have to travel after 10am?
