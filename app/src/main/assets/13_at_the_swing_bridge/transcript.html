Transcript

Do you have a railcard?

Erica: Hi, I’d like to get to London on Friday please. What’s the cheapest way?

Ticket officer: Coming back the same day?

Erica: I’m not sure. Does it make a difference?

Ticket officer: Day return is £15.50. Open return is £22.00.

Erica: Ok. Is it right that with an open return I can come back whenever I want?

Ticket officer: Yes that’s right, as long as it’s within 1 month.

Erica: And I can come back on any train? I don’t have to book a specific train?

Ticket officer: If you book an advance ticket now you have to book your train. You have to come back at the time indicated on your ticket and sit in your allocated seat. But you do save a lot of money by booking ahead.

Erica: Ok. So I’ll book a day return now. Leaving at 10:15 and returning at 19:43. I will have my daughter with me as well. She’s two years old. Will we get any concessions?

Ticket officer: I’m afraid you won’t. But she will. Children under 5 travel free. Have you got a railcard at all?

Erica: No I haven’t. How do I get one of those?

Ticket officer: I can recommend a Network Rail card. You get 30% off your fare on regional trains if you travel after 10:00am. You pay just £30 for the card so you can make significant savings if you’re a regular traveller.

Erica: Great, I have more travel plans this month, so it would be smart to get a railcard now, wouldn’t it?

Ticket officer: It certainly would. All you need to do is fill out this form. Let me take you through your itinerary in the meantime. So outbound journey – depart 10:15, arrive 10:50 at London Euston. Return 19:43… unfortunately due to planned engineering works you’ll need to alight at Bletchley. There’s a replacement bus service between Bletchley and Milton Keynes Central.

Erica: Oh I see. I think that’ll be fine though. I live near Bletchley so we’ll get off there. Thanks. Here is the form. And £30 for the card. Do I get the discount straight away?

Ticket officer: You certainly do. It’s £40.85 altogether please. If you’d just like to pop your card in the machine….
